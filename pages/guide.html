Introduction

Welcome to NovaOTP, a lightweight and secure two-factor authentication (2FA) manager built directly into your browser. This documentation guides you through setting up and optimizing your security experience.

info Pro Tip

NovaOTP works entirely offline by default. Your secrets are stored in your browser's local storage and only leave your device if you explicitly use the GitHub Sync feature.

Manual Entry

The most reliable way to add an account when you have the secret seed provided by the service provider.

  • Open Settings and find the Manual Entry tab.
  • Account Name: Usually your email address or username.
  • Issuer: The name of the service (e.g., Facebook, Discord).
  • Secret Key: Paste the Base32 string. Whitespace is automatically handled.

Google Import

Migrate your existing codes from Google Authenticator in seconds.

  • Open Google Authenticator on your phone.
  • Go to Settings → Transfer accounts → Export accounts.
  • Select your accounts and generate the QR code.
  • Scan the QR to get the otpauth-migration:// URL.
  • Paste this URL into the Google Import tab in NovaOTP Settings.

Editing Accounts

Need to update a label or fix a secret key? Use our dedicated editor modal for precise control.

  • Click the Pencil icon at the top of the Settings panel.
  • Select the account you wish to modify from the list.
  • Update any field and click Save Changes.
  • Changes are immediately reflected in the main view and current OTP codes.

Reordering List

Organize your accounts to prioritize your most frequently used services.

drag_indicator Visual Handle

Look for the ⠿ (Drag Handle) on the left side of each account card in the main list.
  • Click and hold the ⠿ handle of an account.
  • Drag the card to your preferred position in the list.
  • Release to drop. NovaOTP automatically saves the new order instantly.
  • Reordering works in real-time even during active search filtering.

Deleting Data

Cleaning up your dashboard is simple but requires confirmation to prevent accidents.

report_problem Dangerous Action

Deleting an account is permanent. Ensure you have the backup secret or the 2FA deactivated on the service provider before proceed.
  • Navigate to the Delete tab inside the Settings modal.
  • Toggle the checkboxes for the accounts you wish to remove.
  • Use Select All at the bottom for quick mass-deletion.
  • Click Delete Selected and confirm via the secure popup.

GitHub Backup

Never lose your codes again. Sync them securely to your own GitHub account using Private Gists.

warning Safety First

Backup data is encrypted locally with your Backup Password before being uploaded. NovaOTP developers can never see your data.
  • Generate a Personal Access Token (PAT) on GitHub with the gist scope.
  • Set a strong Backup Password. You must remember this password to restore!
  • Click Backup. NovaOTP will create or find a Gist named "NovaOTP Secure Backup Data".

Secure Encryption

We use industry-standard AES-256-GCM encryption for your backups.

  • PBKDF2 Derivation: Your password is never stored; it is used to derive a high-entropy key with 100k iterations and a unique salt.
  • Zero Knowledge: No one but you can decrypt your backup file, not even GitHub or the extension authors.
  • Multi-Password Support: Each unique password generates a distinct filename (e.g., nova_otp_xxxx.txt), allowing you to keep multiple independent backups in one Gist.
